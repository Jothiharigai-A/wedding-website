💙 Our Love Story – A Journey from Text to Together Forever 💙

It all began with a simple message on Instagram — August 8, 2023 📱💬 — the first text that would change everything. 🩵🫂💫

From that moment, she had a little secret wish in her heart: "If this is meant to be, I want a proposal from him within two weeks." And destiny heard her. ✨🌌
Just a month later, on September 7, 2023, he proposed 💍 — and without a moment’s hesitation, she said “OK” 💞 That simple, heartfelt moment sparked the beginning of a love story they would write together. 📖❤️
📍Their first in-person meeting was on October 12, 2023, at a quiet park 💙🌳🕊️ — a day filled with nervous smiles and soft glances 😊💫 But love💙, as it often does, refused to wait. On October 21, unable to bear the distance, he rushed all the way from Solavanthan to Paramakudi, just to see her at the Paramakudi railway station 🚉💌 That day proved — this wasn’t just a love story, it was a connection written in the stars ✨🌠
🌺 Then came a sacred milestone — January 21, 2024, they visited Alagar Kovil in Madurai with their families 💙👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 He took a selfie from afar 🤳 — and there she was, a tiny, beautiful figure in the photo, like a miniature doll framed by fate 🧸💖🌄 Moved by the memory, they visited again — this time on January 20, 2024, as a couple 👫💫
✨💤 And then came a special night — February 12, 2024 — their first overnight family trip to Palani Kovil 🛕🌌 Laughter, shared moments, and togetherness stitched their bond even stronger. 🪡❤️
As days passed, their love only grew — strong, unshakable, unstoppable 💪💖 And like every great love story, theirs had challenges too 🥺 But together, they faced every struggle, every “no”, every tear 💧— until the families finally said YES 🙌👨‍👩‍👧‍👦
🗓️ The official visits followed: 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 His family visited her home on February 9, 2025 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Her family visited his on February 23, 2025
🎉 And then came the happiest decision of their lives — 💍 Engagement fixed on June 8, 2025 💒 Wedding day sealed for August 29, 2025
🎁 The celebrations began early — 🛍️ They started wedding shopping on April 10, 2025 👗 Found their perfect engagement outfits on April 30, 2025
✨💑 And now, here they are — on their big engagement day 💍 smiling at each other, hearts full of memories 💖, telling their story to the world 🌍📸💙
“This is just the beginning,💙” they say, “…because our love story is still being written — with every hug, every day, every laugh, and every ‘I love you’.” 💌🫂💞
